 Biography Of Nadine Mahmoud

Hello! I'm Nadine, a 20-year-old from Palestine, currently living in the city of the Birthplace of Christ as a Software Engineering Student.








My academic Life

My academic journey began at Talitha Kumi, where I laid the foundation for my education. Then I made the transition to Bethlehem Girls High School for my high school years. And With my outstanding high school performance, I decided to enroll in Bethlehem University, to pursue Software Engineering. Currently, I find myself in the midst of my third year at Bethlehem University.

Throughout my academic journey, I have not only excelled in my studies but have also actively engaged in student organizations like MUN at Talitha Kumi School and the ACCESS program with Amideast (2018-2020), These experiences have provided me with a well-rounded education and a strong personality for a successful future.








My University

Bethlehem University is not just a place of higher education; it's a sanctuary of knowledge nestled in the heart of a city with an ancient and rich history. It's where the vibrant hum of eager minds and the echoes of wisdom from generations past blend harmoniously.

Like a bridge between tradition and innovation, this institution is a beacon of enlightenment, fostering the next generation of scholars, dreamers, and innovators. Bethlehem University is creating a unique atmosphere where education flourishes amidst the echoes of time.








My sports

I don't have a favorite sport, but I enjoy staying active through activities like walking. It provides me with a great opportunity to clear my mind and connect with nature.

Despite my lazy mind, I appreciate the importance of persevering and staying open to new experiences, even if it means overcoming challenges in the process.








My Achievement

My journey of achievement has been an exhilarating ascent, marked by numerous milestones that I hold dear. Graduating from Amideast in 2020 was my first step toward achievement. The subsequent year, I emerged as a senior in high school, where I showcased my academic prowess with a remarkable 98.7% in the 12th grade. As a third-year Software Engineering student at my university, I've continued to shine, consistently becoming at the top of the Dean's List.

In 2022 and 2023, I tested my problem-solving mettle in prestigious coding competitions - PCPC and Hebron Code Jam - embracing challenges and honing my programming skills. Furthermore, the pinnacle of my achievements was achieving second place in the College of Applied Sciences for the year 2022-2023. These accomplishments not only define my journey but also inspire me to continue pushing boundaries and reaching for even greater heights in the years to come.








My City

Bethlehem, a city nestled within the heart of the West Bank, is a captivating tapestry of history, spirituality, and resilient beauty. Its ancient streets are adorned with stories etched in the very stones, and the echoes of generations past can be heard in the whispers of olive trees that have stood the test of time.

Home to the Church of the Nativity, this city is a sanctuary of pilgrimage for the faithful, where the aura of spirituality is as palpable as the golden light that bathes its landscapes at dawn and dusk. The centuries-old charm of Bethlehem, with its labyrinthine markets and timeless architecture, is a testament to the enduring spirit of the place.








My Favorite

Music is not just a passion; it's a thread that weaves the tapestry of my life, connecting me to memories and emotions in a way nothing else can. My favorite melody, though, isn't something I've discovered on my own; it's the symphony of my dad's playlist, a treasure trove of tunes he carefully curated over the years.

With every note and every lyric, I find myself transported to moments spent with him, and it's as though his presence lingers in each chord and verse, even after his passing. His impeccable taste in music was a legacy he left behind, a gift that keeps on giving, making me feel as though he's right here beside me, sharing the songs that once brought us so much joy.








My Role Model

My father, a beacon of resilience and compassion, was more than just a parent; he was my ultimate role model. As he was the executive director of a Palestinian trade union, his unwavering dedication and tenacity in fighting for the rights of workers left an indelible mark on my perception of justice. His stubbornness, which emerged from a genuine desire to combat injustice, especially in the face of the Zionist occupation, was the fuel that propelled him forward in his lifelong battle for fairness. His journey through education was arduous, marked by interruptions due to imprisonment, yet he consistently persisted.

He cherished the value of education, deeply aware of its transformative power amidst the challenging circumstances of occupation. Despite the struggles, he remained a caring and nurturing father, showering us with love and instilling within us a profound sense of compassion. The generosity he displayed in helping others, driven solely by his abhorrence for injustice, is a testament to the greatness of his heart and the enduring legacy of his compassion that lives on within us.
